S-Space

When a player lands on a "S" space, they shall be safe from all actions. (eg. if an enemy player lands is in the act of taking a point from a player,you will not be able to be targeted)

N-Space

When a player lands on a "N" space, all player's shall have their points reset to 0.

Z-Space

When a player lands on a Z space, all players shall move back X spaces, determined by the number rolled on the dice. Only the player who rolled the dice will have the ending space effect applied.(eg. If you rolled a 4, and landed on a "Z" space, all players will move back 4 spaces. If you now land on a -2, you will lose 2 points. All other players ONLY have their position effected.)

Details

This project, OVERTHRONE is a party style board game with a set amount of turns determined by the player(s). Your main objective is to end the game with the greatest number of points. Players will roll a die to determine the order of play. Each player will take turns rolling dice. The number rolled on the dice is the total number of spaces the player will move. After a player moves, they will preform an action based on the space that they land on, and then that player's turn is ended. After the last player's turn has ended, a random minigame will start, giving all player's the opportunity to receive more points through their skills. If a player reaches an endpoint on the map, their position will be reset at a random stating position.

GP-Space

When a player lands on a "GP-space", they shall give X number of points to a player of their choice. (eg, GP = Give 1 point, GP2 = Give 2 points)

TP-Space

When a player lands on a "TP-space", they shall take X number of points from a player of their choice. (eg, TP = Take 1 point, TP2 = Take 2 points)
